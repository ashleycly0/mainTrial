STUDENT DEMO TASK

Design a Smart Cup

In this activity, you will act as an innovator. Your mission is to improve an ordinary cup and turn it into a Smart Cup by generating new ideas, functions, and features through the METS Extension process.

1

Think from a normal cup

Start with a basic cup and imagine how it can be upgraded, redesigned, or made smarter.

2

Explore new possibilities

Consider materials, temperature control, reminders, portability, health tracking, cleaning, and more.

3

Build connections

Link your ideas together to discover meaningful relationships and practical smart features.

4

Create a final concept

At the end, you will form a complete Smart Cup concept that can move to the next stage.

What you will do

You will go through 4 Extension levels to expand your idea step by step.

Level 2.1 — Divergence

  • Generate many characteristics, values, and usage scenarios
  • Example: insulated, self-heating, gym use, kids use

Level 2.2 — Correlative

  • Connect different ideas together
  • Example: smart lid → hydration reminder → better user habits

Level 2.3 / 2.4

  • Implication: build IF → THEN logic
  • Opening-up: combine ideas into one final Smart Cup concept

Your goal

Create a Smart Cup idea that is useful, creative, and suitable for students’ daily life.

Read the task brief, then press Start.
