DEMO TASK

Design a Smart Cup

In this demo, you will complete the full innovation journey of creating a Smart Cup. You will begin by understanding the product, then expand ideas, develop a stronger concept, and finally choose the best result.

Task Overview

Your mission is to turn an ordinary cup into a meaningful and creative Smart Cup concept. To do this, you will go through four main stages: Model, Extension, Transformation, and Selection. Each stage helps you improve your idea step by step until you reach a final solution.

Token System (Will be launched in the Next Version)

QUIZ → TOKEN → HINT

During the Game, you can earn tokens by answering small questions about the METS method. These tokens are shared across all four levels: Model, Extension, Transformation, and Selection.

How to earn tokens

Each Extension part contains a short question. When you answer correctly, you earn a token.

How to use tokens

Tokens are used to unlock hints. A hint can only be unlocked when you have enough tokens.

Shared across 4 parts

If you earn a token in Divergence, you can save it and use it later in Correlative, Implication, or Opening-up.

Important rule

If your token count is 0, the hint button cannot be used until you earn more tokens.

The 4 Stages of the Task

Read the stages below so you know what you will do in the full Smart Cup activity.

1

Model

In the Model stage, you first understand the original object: the cup. You identify its basic features, structure, functions, and current use.

2

Extension

In the Extension stage, you expand the idea of the cup by exploring new possibilities and relationships.

  • Divergence: generate many new ideas and possibilities
  • Correlative: connect related features and functions
  • Implication: think about cause-and-effect logic
  • Opening-up: combine ideas into bigger concept directions
3

Transformation

In the Transformation stage, you turn your extended ideas into a clearer product concept. You reshape your ideas into something more practical, understandable, and usable.

  • Refine the Smart Cup concept
  • Organize the main functions and design direction
  • Make the idea more realistic and meaningful
4

Selection

In the Selection stage, you evaluate and choose the best final concept. This is where you decide which Smart Cup idea is strongest.

  • Compare different ideas
  • Judge usefulness, creativity, and suitability
  • Select the best final Smart Cup solution

Your final goal

Complete all four stages and produce a creative, useful, and student-friendly Smart Cup that can be presented as your final innovation result.
